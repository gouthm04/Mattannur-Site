Mattannur — Airport City with a Heartbeat

Not your average town page: spicy snacks, greener than your WhatsApp DP, and a runway just down the road.

Kannur International Airport (IATA: CNN) is right here On the Mattannur–Iritty route to Western Ghats Chenda beats by Padma Shri Mattannur Sankarankutty Marar Palukachippara viewpoint sunsets

Pronounced MA-ta-nnur (IPA: /mʌt̪ːänur/). If Kerala is God’s Own Country, Mattannur is the gate with a drum solo.

Show nerdy etymology / extra

You’ll also see it spelled Mattannoor. It sits between Kannur, Thalassery and Iritty, acting as a road hub towards Kodagu (Coorg).

See & Do

  • Palukachippara Viewpoint — 7–10 km from town; sunrise/airport views; mini‑trek vibes.
  • Pazhassi Dam & Garden — scenic picnic spot on the Mattannur–Iritty route; boating & amphitheatre.
  • Airport Spotting — café run + watch take‑offs on clear evenings.
  • Temple Theyyam circuits — seasonal, dramatic, loud, unforgettable.
“Mattannur town is very nearest to Kannur International Airport.” — District Portal

Culture = Rhythm

The chenda is our unofficial town logo. Padma Shri Mattannur Sankarankutty Marar put Mattannur on the percussion map with thayambaka and panchari melam.

Festival season? Expect goosebumps, percussion walls, and happy chaos.

Snackology

  1. Banana chips (inevitable).
  2. Malabar biryani (inevitable x2).
  3. Tea that solves 60% of your problems.

Pro tip: carry cash at tiny huts; UPI works at most places but mountains love dead zones.

Getting Here

  • By Air: Land at Kannur International Airport (CNN) — it’s in Mattannur (≈3–5 km from town core).
  • By Road: Buses towards Mysuru/Bengaluru and to Kannur/Thalassery pass via town. Easy taxi/auto from airport.
  • By Rail: Alight at Kannur or Thalassery stations; connect by bus/taxi to Mattannur.

FAQ (real questions we asked ourselves)

Is Mattannur actually an “airport city” or is that just hype?

Not hype. The airport is within a few km of town limits and officially located in Mattannur.

What’s one unusual flex?

Spot planes and trek a hill the same evening: Palukachippara.

Any day‑trip ideas?

Pazhassi Dam & Garden (east), Muzhappilangad Drive‑in Beach (west), and cinnamon history at Anjarakandy.
